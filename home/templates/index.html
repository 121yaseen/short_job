About Us

Short Jobs is the world's first freelancing and crowdsourcing platform for unskilled users and works. We connect over 38,728,698 employers and employeers globally from over 147 countries, regions and territories. Through our marketplace, employers can hire workers to do unskilled work in their neighbourhood areas and freebies can earn some extra bucks. We have one ultimate goal : connect clients to our community of freelancers who are available to hire by the hour or project
